An exceptional cloud-gaming laptop

ASUS Chromebook Vibe CX34 Flip is engineered with a striking aesthetic and powerful components for non-stop cloud gaming — with an immersive 16:10 144 Hz display, accurate anti-ghosting RGB backlit keyboard and ultra-fast WiFi 6E technology.

  • Fast, fluid visuals

    Pack a 16:10 144 Hz high-refresh-rate WUXGA touchscreen display to deliver vivid, true-to-life imagery, without blur or lag — drawing you deeper into the virtual world. It also boasts a three-sided NanoEdge panel to maximize the display area and a higher screen-to-body ratio, making visuals even more immersive.

  • Next-level gaming experience

    ASUS Chromebook Vibe CX34 Flip delivers cloud-gaming freedom with instant access to NVIDIA GeForce NOW and Xbox Cloud Gaming. Instantly access 1000+ games — no installs, no downloads, no hassle. It is ready for every online battle!

  • Gaming-optimized design

    The striking orange-accented WASD keycaps and accurate anti-ghosting function provides quick and intuitive gameplay. Plus, the 4-zone RGB backlit keyboard creates a unique look for gamers. The WiFi 6E technology ensures greater speed and stability to stay connected anywhere.
